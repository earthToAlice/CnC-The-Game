Cowboys and Cookies: The Game! is a text-based story rpg that sends you on a dazzling adventure with the members of the Cowboys and Cookies discord server. But beware, for danger lurks ahead! On your path to find the legendary Baker's Cowboy Hat, you will find enemies trying to spread the will of their master, Negative Vibes! Using the power of positivity (and any weapons you find along the way) you will retake the Baker's Cowboy Hat from Negative Vibes and restore the power of Sheriff Cowboy!
